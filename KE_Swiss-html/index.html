VISION


Our vision is striving to address poverty eradicating projects
to improve the welfare of Kenyans in Kenya by soliciting for aids,
grants and resources rendered by self- initiatives, donors, sponsors,
well-wishers and associates of Kenya.


MISSION


Our mission is to unite Kenyans, foster and maintain social
integration with other nationalities as well as the well-being of Kenyans
living in Switzerland and in the European Union (EU) countries.

About Us

We the Kenya Swiss Connection are an Organization open to sound mind, able or disabled individual adults, Kenyan and non- Kenyan residents of Switzerland and European Union (EU) countries as well as immediate diaspora residents of Kenya, irrespective of their political opinion, gender, nationality, creed, colour or race.

Our purpose of Kenya Swiss Connection is to unite Kenyans, foster and maintain social integration with other nationalities as well as the well-being of Kenyans living in Switzerland and in the European Union (EU) countries.

The association establishes and promotes social, cultural, educational and economic values between Kenya and Switzerland by facilitating a transparent and accountable running of our affairs.
The association pursues to fostering interaction with other legal bodies and still retain its status of being a non-profit and non-political.
